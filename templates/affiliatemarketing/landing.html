FAQ
Common Questions
Is there an approval process?
Yes. Every application is manually reviewed by our team to ensure partners meet quality standards. This protects both the program's integrity and your earnings. Approval typically takes 24–48 hours.
How is my EasyPaisa account verified?
You submit your registered account holder name and EasyPaisa number during application. Before your first payout, we perform an internal cross-check to ensure accuracy. We never ask for your PIN or password.
When do I get paid?
You can request a payout any time your available balance crosses PKR {{ settings.minimum_payout }}. Requests are processed within 3–5 business days via EasyPaisa.
What qualifies as a conversion?
A conversion is recorded when a referred user (who clicked your link within the {{ settings.cookie_duration_days }}-day window) purchases any paid plan. You earn {{ settings.commission_rate }}% of the plan price as commission.
Can I refer myself?
No. Self-referrals are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account suspension and forfeiture of all earned commissions.
Do commissions expire?
No. As long as your partner account is active, commissions remain in your balance indefinitely until you request a payout.
