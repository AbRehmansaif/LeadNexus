How the ROI Dashboard Works

The LeadNexus Intelligence engine continuously monitors your outreach channels (Email and LinkedIn) and cross-references link clicks with conversion events on your website. By analyzing this data stream, it generates a real-time visualization of your sales funnel, allowing you to see the exact value of every click and the performance of every outreach template.
